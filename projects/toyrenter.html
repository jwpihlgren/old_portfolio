Toy Renter App

Picture and GIF by J.Pihlgren. Press the gif to turn it off or on.
Description

The Toy Renter app is the result of a Home Exam in Mobile Computing. It allows parents to rent or lend toys from other parents, instead of buying new toys. The app allows parents to add items to lend and accept rent requests or browse available items and make rent requests. Payment has been put outside of the app, but depending on the business model, in app payment could be arranged.

The project
What
An interactive smart phone app prototype for parents to rent and lend children's toys.
Why
The 'buy and throw' paradigm of today doesn't align with the environmentally aware modern parents. This app allows toys to be reused, for a price that can benefit both the lender and the renter.
Aim
Provide parents with a way to rent and lend childrens toys.
The target group
  • Parents of younger children
  • Ages 18 - 60
