Noisemeter

Graphical Design, left, by Sjoerd Hendricks and Erik Bennerhed . Digital implementation in Wear OS, right, by Tommy Olsson, Robin Edquist, Daniel Illipe and Joachim Pihlgren
Description

The noisemeter is the result of a project in mobile computing. It's a partially implemented conceptual design which aims to decrease fatique due to noise exposure. Here we worked in such a fashion that the design team produced digital designs which were handed over to the programming team. When an implementation was finished, it was evaluated and adjustments were made.
