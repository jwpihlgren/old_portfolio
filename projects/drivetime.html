Description

The AI Quiz Game is the product of a project in Human Centered Design where we were supposed to design services for drivers of Autonomous Vehicles of different levels . Here we created an AI driven quiz game aimed at helping the driver maintaining situational awareness while out-of-loop in a level 3 AV. The concept was then tested using a combination of wizard of Oz and enactment and evaulated subjectively by a potential target group.

The project
What
Concept for an AI driven quiz game.
Why
Drivers of level 3 Autonomous Vehicles are constantly switching between driving themselves and letting the car drive. When the car drives, drivers run the risk of losing situational awareness as they conduct other tasks than driving. If the car suddenly gives back control to the user and they have lost their situational awareness, they may make poor decisions.
Aim
Help drivers in maintaining their situational awareness by providing them with entertainment that keeps them situated in their environment and keeping eyes on the road.
The target group
  • Drivers of Autonomous Vehicles, level 3
  • Ages 18-70
