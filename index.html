About me

As a child I wanted to work with computers. I got my first computer when I was five and I've never let them go. Later on, I wanted to be a psychologist, always fashinated with the behaviors of humans. So when I was presented with the scientific area of Cognitive science, the combination of IT and pyschology, I knew I had found my match. It was here that I was then introduced to Interaction Design and the challanges of shaping products that are both useful and used.
